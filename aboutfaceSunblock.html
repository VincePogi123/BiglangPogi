Facial Sunscreen

Usage

Protects the skin from harmful UV rays, prevents sunburn, and reduces the risk of skin cancer and premature aging.

Ingredients

Zinc Oxide: Physical (mineral) sunscreen that blocks UV rays. Titanium Dioxide: Another mineral sunscreen that reflects UV radiation. Avobenzone: Chemical sunscreen that absorbs UV rays. Octinoxate: Provides UVB protection.

Precautions

Apply liberally and reapply every 2 to 3 hours, especially when outdoors. Use broad spectrum sunscreen with SPF 30 or higher for better protection.
