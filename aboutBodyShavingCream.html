Body Shaving Cream

Usage

Wet your face with warm water to soften hair. Apply a small amount of shaving cream to your hands or directly on your face. Lather it up, covering the areas to be shaved. Shave as usual, then rinse your face with water.

Ingredients

Aqua (Water), Glycerin, Stearic Acid, Sodium Hydroxide, Fragrance, Various moisturizing agents and preservatives

Precautions

For external use only; avoid contact with eyes. Discontinue use if irritation occurs. Keep out of reach of children and store in a cool, dry place.
