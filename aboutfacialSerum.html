Facial Serum

Usage

A concentrated treatment that addresses specific skin concerns like anti aging, hydration

Ingredients

Vitamin C: An antioxidant that brightens skin and reduces dark spots. Retinol: Promotes cell turnover and reduces fine lines and wrinkles. Niacinamide: Reduces redness and boosts skin barrier function. Hyaluronic Acid: Provides deep hydration.

Precautions

Introduce retinol gradually, as it can cause irritation. Vitamin C serums should be stored in a cool, dark place to prevent oxidation. Always patch test to check for allergic reactions.
