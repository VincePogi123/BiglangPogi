Body Moisturizer

Usage

Helps to hydrate the skin and lock in moisture, keeping it soft and supple.

Ingredients

Hyaluronic Acid: Hydrates the skin by retaining moisture. Glycerin: Draws water into the skin to maintain hydration. Ceramides: Help restore the skin barrier. Shea Butter: Nourishes and softens the skin.

Precautions

Avoid heavy, oil based moisturizers if you have oily or acne prone skin.
