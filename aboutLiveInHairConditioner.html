Hair Live in Conditioner

Usage

Adds extra hydration and protection without needing to rinse it out, helping to detangle and smooth hair

Ingredients

Argan Oil: Adds shine and protects against heat damage. Coconut Milk: Nourishes and hydrates. Proteins (e.g., Keratin): Strengthens and repairs hair strands. Aloe Vera: Soothes and moisturizes.

Precautions

Use sparingly on fine hair to avoid weighing it down. Avoid applying to the scalp if you have oily hair; focus on the ends.
