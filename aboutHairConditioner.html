Hair Conditioner

Usage

Moisturizes and softens hair, making it easier to detangle and more manageable.

Ingredients

Silicones (e.g., Dimethicone): Smooth the hair and reduce frizz. Keratin: Strengthens hair by replenishing proteins. Coconut Oil: Moisturizes and helps with hair damage. Panthenol (Vitamin B5): Hydrates and adds shine.

Precautions

Avoid heavy conditioners if you have fine hair, as they can weigh it down. Silicones can cause buildup over time, so use a clarifying shampoo occasionally.
