Body Wash

Usage

Wet your body with water. Apply a small amount of body wash to your hands or a loofah. Lather it onto your skin, focusing on areas needing extra cleansing. Rinse thoroughly with water.

Ingredients

Aqua (Water) Sodium Laureth Sulfate Cocamidopropyl Betaine Glycerin Menthol (for cooling effect) Various surfactants and moisturizing agents Fragrance Preservatives (e.g., Sodium Benzoate) (Exact ingredients may vary by product version; always check the label.)

Precautions

For external use only; avoid contact with eyes. If irritation occurs, discontinue use. Keep out of reach of children. Store in a cool, dry place. Always perform a patch test before using a new product, especially if you have sensitive skin.
