Hair Shampoo

Usage

Helps to hydrate the skin and lock in moisture, keeping it soft and supple

Ingredients

Sulfates (e.g., Sodium Lauryl Sulfate): Cleansing agents that create lather and remove oils. Aloe Vera: Soothes and hydrates the scalp. Tea Tree Oil: Helps with dandruff and scalp irritation. Argan Oil: Adds shine and moisturizes hair.

Precautions

Avoid shampoos with sulfates if you have dry or curly hair, as they can strip natural oils. Look for moisturizing ingredients like glycerin or natural oils for dry hair.
