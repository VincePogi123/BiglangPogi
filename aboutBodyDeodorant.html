Body Deodorant

Usage

shake the canister well. Hold it about 15 cm away from your underarms. Spray evenly for full coverage. Allow it to dry before dressing.

Ingredients

Butane, Isobutane, Propane (propellants) Aluminum Chlorohydrate (active ingredient) Cyclopentasiloxane Fragrance Water Other skin conditioning agents (Ingredient lists may vary; always check the label for specifics.)

Precautions

For external use only; avoid contact with eyes and broken skin. Do not apply to freshly shaved or irritated skin. Keep away from heat and flames. Store in a cool, dry place and keep out of reach of children.
