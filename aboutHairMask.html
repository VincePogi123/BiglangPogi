Hair Mask

Usage

Provides deep conditioning to repair and nourish damaged or dry hair.

Ingredients

Shea Butter: Deeply moisturizes and softens hair. Avocado Oil: Nourishes hair and promotes growth. Honey: Retains moisture and adds shine. Olive Oil: Strengthens and adds elasticity.

Precautions

Use only once or twice a week to avoid product buildup. Rinse thoroughly to prevent residue from making the hair look greasy.
