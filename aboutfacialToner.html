Facial Toner

Usage

Applied after cleansing to balance the skin's pH and prepare it for moisturizing and other treatments.

Ingredients

Astringent that tightens pores and reduces inflammation. Rose Water: Hydrates and soothes the skin. Glycolic Acid: Exfoliates and improves skin texture by removing dead skin cells.

Precautions

Choose an alcohol-free toner to avoid drying out the skin. Sensitive skin types should avoid strong astringents and opt for calming ingredients like aloe vera or chamomile.
